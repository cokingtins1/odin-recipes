Creamed Swiss Chard

Ingredients:

  • (2) Bunches of Swiss Chard
  • (1) Cup Whole Milk
  • (1) Cup Heavy Cream
  • (2) Cloves of Garlic
  • (15g) AP Flour
  • Pinch of Salt

Instructions

  1. Cut leaves from chard stems and dice into 1'' segments. Dice stems small and add to dutch oven
  2. Heat over medium heat with pinch of salt and sweat for 2-3 mins.
  3. Add garlic and flour and cook for additional 1 minute
  4. Add milks and cook to reduce for 25-30 mins.
